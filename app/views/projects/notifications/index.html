Notifications

Overview

Previous User research and communications with our Users, discovered the need to see what applications require them to take action, in a clearer and quicker way than is currently available to them.

Project team

  • Andy Carr - Interaction design
  • Lauren Thomas - Interaction design
  • James Thomas - Content design
  • Jane James - User research
  • Anxo Cereijo - User research

Design

User research based recommended design

  • Design - This has come out of 4 rounds of user research and testing. It combines all of the actionable insights and learnings.
  • Tabs - applications are now separated in to All, Requires action and HMLR to review
  • Standalone page - applications open in their own pages, rather than in an expanded accordion
  • Standalone page content - content is placed in to accordions, to allow power users to only display the information they need
  • Notes - a new section that allows users and colleagues to post notes on applications
  • Visual progress - this dynamic graphic helped more junior users in particular
  • Reply to requisition - a dedicated area complete wtih 3 simple steps to help guide the user through the replying process.
  • Share application - users can press a button and copy the direct link to the application to share with colleagues or use in their own tracking documents
